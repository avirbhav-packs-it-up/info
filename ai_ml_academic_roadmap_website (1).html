Physics Requirement Myth — Clarified

The Myth

A common misconception is that Physics (and Chemistry) are mandatory for AI, Computer Science, or Data Science admissions worldwide.

In reality, universities prioritize mathematics, algorithms, and problem‑solving ability — not physical science content — for AI‑focused degrees.

The Reality

AI / ML operates on abstract systems: probability theory, linear algebra, optimization, statistics, and computation.

These skills are evaluated far more directly through advanced mathematics exams, competitive tests, and coding‑based assessments.

Where Physics Is Fulfilled

When required or beneficial, AP Physics C: Mechanics fully satisfies the mathematical physics expectation.

This course uses calculus‑based reasoning aligned with engineering and quantitative programs — without diverting focus from AI‑critical subjects.
